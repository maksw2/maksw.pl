radminBot Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05.07.2024

radminBot ("we," "us," or "our") respects the privacy of its users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use radminBot, a Discord bot, and how we use that information.

What Information Does radminBot Collect?

radminBot does not collect or store any personal information from users. This includes but is not limited to:

How Does radminBot Use Collected Information?

Since radminBot does not collect any user information, there is no information to be used.

How Does radminBot Share Information?

radminBot does not share any user information with third parties.

While radminBot does not collect user data, we strive to maintain the security of any information that is transmitted through the Discord platform. However, no website or online service can guarantee complete security.
